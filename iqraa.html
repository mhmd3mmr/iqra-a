What is iQara’a

iQra'a is the first affordable, innovative, interactive no–formal educational system, specially developed for non-Arabic speakers to teach the Arabic language, in addition to the Local dialect language and the culture of the country, through an interactive mobile application, the application's function is to display educational content (educational videos, pictures, audio recordings, challenges).

What Make us special

The aim is to enable the non-Arabic-speaker to communicate and interact using the Arabic language through Arabic content designed by specialists in teaching the Arabic language, and the votes are recorded by the sons of society to ensure an understanding of the dialect and culture.
